My University

Bahria University, Islamabad Campus, Computer Science Department is part of School of Engineering and Applied Sciences. The department offers undergraduate degree programs in Computer Science and Information Technology and graduate degree programs in Computer Science, Data Science, Information Security, Telecom and Networking, and Mathematics.
The Deparment of Computer Science is a vibrant team of professionals and researchers working on leading computing domains of Articial Intelligence, Blockchain, Cybersecurity, Data Science, Networks, and Software Development. We take a scientific and forward-looking approach to computing and this is characterized by our high-quality teaching and addition of innovative and contemporary courses to the cirriculum.
Our primary goal educational goal is to prepare students for careers in academia or in industry.
