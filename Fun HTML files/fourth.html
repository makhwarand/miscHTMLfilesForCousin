Bahria University Islamabad Campus

Bahria University is a Federally Character Public Sector University. The princple seat of Bahria University is at Islamabad and campuses are at Islamabad, Karachi, and Lahore. Bahria University was established by the Pakistan Navy in 2000, and since then it has steadily grown into one of the leading higher educational institutions of Pakistan. It plays a major role in grooming future leaders who can have a positive difference to the world around them. Bahria is a comprehensive university having multidisplinary programs that includes Health Sciences, Engineering Sciences, Computer Sciences, Management Sciences, Social Sciences, Law, Earth and Environmental Sciences, Psychology and Maritime Sciences.

The title we aim for all this to take place in a university environment that is safe and friendly, and the same time disciplined, as envisaged by Quaid e Azam. Because we feel that along with studies it is equally important to develop the good old values of character, decency, honesty, and consideration of one another.
